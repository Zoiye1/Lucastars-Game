## Storyline

Roy is a streamer with anger issues. During his streams, he is regularly stream sniped by fans. One day, he had enough and tracked down a fan, beating them up. He was arrested and taken to a psychiatric institution. But this isn't a normal institution; experiments are conducted on the patients. He is locked up there and wants to escape. It can end in different ways for Roy, depending on the choices he makes during his escape.
