## Gameplay

In this game, you can:  
 - Navigate through the game using arrow keys.  

 - Perform certain actions with items.  
    - Such as examining, using, combining, etc.  
    - You can use items on the environment.  

 - Talk to NPCs to get information.  
    - You can also ask NPCs questions; they can help or block you.  

 - Gain information about the environment by looking into camera rooms.  

 - Use items on NPCs.  

 - Go to different rooms.  
    - Use items to open them.  
    - You can also talk to NPCs to open them.  

 - You have an inventory where you can store items.
